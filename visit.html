Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours every hour (70 NOK/person, includes printed guide).
For groups of 6+, please contact us for a private tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair ramps, audio guides, and braille display signs.

Food and Drink

The café offers light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop has gifts, activity packs, and museum memorabilia to continue your science fun at home.
